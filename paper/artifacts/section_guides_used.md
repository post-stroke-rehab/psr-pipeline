# Research-Paper-Writing-Skills Trace

This manuscript was drafted against the external `Master-cai/Research-Paper-Writing-Skills` workflow rather than written as a one-pass summary.

## Workflow Applied

1. **Mini-outline first:** `mini_outline.md` was written before prose drafting.
2. **Section-specific drafting:** the manuscript used the skill's guidance for Abstract, Introduction, Related Work, Method, Experiments, Conclusion, and final paper review.
3. **Claim-evidence discipline:** `claim_evidence_map.md` was maintained while writing Abstract and Introduction so unsupported claims could be weakened.
4. **Reverse outlining:** `reverse_outline.md` was produced after drafting to check topic sentence to thesis alignment.
5. **Adversarial self-review:** `self_review.md` records reviewer-style checks on contribution, clarity, experimental strength, evaluation completeness, and method soundness.

## Practical Interpretation in This Repo

- The skill recommends stronger experimental coverage than this repository currently stores.
- Instead of inventing missing experiments, the manuscript weakens claims and marks missing evidence explicitly.
- The result is a credible repo paper draft that matches current evidence rather than an overstated submission draft.
